FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

First bar run? Everything you need to know, answered here.

Do I actually have to run?
Not at all. You walk the whole thing — cruise the neighborhood at your own pace, bar to bar, drink to drink. It's about having fun, not being fast.
Can I join if I'm under 18?
No. The event involves alcoholic drinks throughout and is strictly 18+. Photo ID is checked at entry, so please bring it.
Is it awkward to come alone?
Not even a little. The games and interactive stops make it easy to meet people — and of course, bringing your whole crew earns group discounts too.
What does the ticket include?
Exclusive drink redemptions at partner bars, the games, and entry to the finish-line party. Exact inclusions and ticket types per event follow the official announcements.
Which ticket types exist, and what's the best deal?
Past editions offered super early bird, early bird, general, duo and group tickets — the earlier you buy, the cheaper it gets, and crews save the most. Final types and prices per event are on our official LINE ticket page.
What if it rains?
The run happens rain or shine. If typhoon-grade weather forces changes, we announce them immediately on our official Instagram and LINE.
I don't speak Chinese — can I still join?
Absolutely. The bar map is easy to follow, and plenty of bar staff and fellow runners speak English. Message us on Instagram if you have questions before the event.
