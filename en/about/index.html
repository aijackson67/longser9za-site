About

What's a "Bar Run"?

A bar run isn't about speed — it's about how much fun you can fit in one night. Since setting off from Taichung's West District in 2024, the run has brought together over 10,000 runners and 170+ partner bar stops. Roam the neighborhood freely, walk into every partner bar for an exclusive event drink, collect stamps, play games — the whole city becomes your bar.
